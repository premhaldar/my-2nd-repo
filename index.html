Welcome to the world Cup History

The first Cricket World Cup was held in 1975, with the West Indies winning the tournament. Since then, the tournament has been held every four years, with a total of 12 editions having taken place as of 2021.The most successful team in World Cup history is Australia, who have won the tournament five times (1987, 1999, 2003, 2007, 2015)..Other teams that have won the tournament include the WestIndies (1975, 1979), India (1983), Pakistan (1992), Sri Lanka (1996), and England (2019). The next World Cup is scheduled to be held in India in 2023.
